1\. Introduction



The Hospital Management System (HMS) is designed to streamline hospital operations by digitizing patient records, appointments, doctor schedules, billing, pharmacy management, and administrative processes.



2\. Problem Statement



Most hospitals still rely on manual processes, which causes:



Long patient waiting times



Misplaced patient records



Difficulty tracking doctor schedules



Slow billing process



High operational costs



Human errors



3\. Objectives



Improve patient experience



Automate hospital daily operations



Reduce administrative workload



Enable fast patient record retrieval



Support accurate billing



Provide secure and centralized data



4\. Stakeholders



Patients



Doctors



Nurses



Receptionists



Pharmacists



Hospital Administrators



IT Staff



5\. Scope



The system includes:



Patient Registration



Appointment Scheduling



Electronic Medical Records (EMR)



Pharmacy Management



Billing \& Insurance



Doctor \& Nurse Management



Laboratory Test Requests



Reporting \& Dashboards



6\. Constraints



Must comply with health data privacy laws



Should work on low-speed machines in some hospitals



Must support role-based access
