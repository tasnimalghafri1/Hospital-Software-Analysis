Hospital Workflow Example

1\. Patient Registration



Patient arrives at reception



Receptionist enters patient details (name, age, gender, phone, address)



System creates a unique Patient ID



2\. Appointment Booking



Receptionist or patient chooses doctor



System checks doctor's availability



Appointment is scheduled



SMS or email confirmation sent



3\. Consultation



Patient visits doctor



Doctor reviews medical history



Doctor records diagnosis and prescribed medicine



4\. Laboratory Tests (If needed)



Doctor requests tests



Lab performs tests



Technician uploads results



Doctor receives notification



5\. Pharmacy



Pharmacist receives prescription



Medicine dispensed



Stock decreases automatically



6\. Billing



System calculates charges



Invoice generated



Patient pays using cash/insurance



7\. Discharge



Final report created



Patient leaves hospital
